Medical-Center-Schema

Tables & Relationships:

* doctors

	*id (PRIMARY KEY)

	*first_name, last_name

*patients

	*id (PRIMARY KEY)

	*first_name, last_name

*visits (Join Table)

	*id (PRIMARY KEY)

	*doctor_id (FOREIGN KEY to doctors)

	*patient_id (FOREIGN KEY to patients)

	*visit_date

*diseases

	*id (PRIMARY KEY)

	*name

	*diagnoses (Join Table)
	
	*id (PRIMARY KEY)

	*visit_id (FOREIGN KEY to visits)

	*disease_id (FOREIGN KEY to diseases)
